Discover

Manicure

A manicure treatment is not only a treatment for natural nails but also for the hands. A manicure consists of filing, shaping of the nail, cuticle treatment, massaging of hands and application of nail polish.

DISCOVER

Spa Pedicure

A pedicure is a way to improve appearance of the feet and nails. It provides a similar service to a manicure. Additionally, our spa pedicure is the ultimate in full body relaxation. Your feet are soaked in a whirlpool while you recline in a massage chair which massages your entire body. Leg care below the knee has become a common and now expected service which includes removal of dead skin cells on the bottom of feet, exfoliation, leg massage with moisturising cream and application of nail polish

DISCOVER

Gel Polish Nails

GEL Polish is for people who want to maintain natural nails. The formulation looks good from day 1 to day 14, but does not appear to damage nail beds. The product is thin and strong enough to be applied similarly to nail polish, but it is cured in a way that gives it great flexibility and durability. It also has the incredible shine associated only with gel nails.

DISCOVER

Dipping Powder

Dip powder nail are somewhere between a regular mani and a fake nail. Instead of using UV rays to seal in your polish, the color comes from a pigmented powder. Between base coats and a sealant, you dip your nails into a little jar of your chosen color ( SNS, Prodip are the two most popular and vetted manufacturers) for a manicure that could last three to four weeks.

DISCOVER

Artificial Acrylic

Tips are glued on the end of your natural nails making them look longer (length of your choice). Then acrylic nails are made by mixing a high quality acrylic powder with liquid, they are placed over the top of your nails properly to make them look thin and natural. They are also long lasting and durable.

DISCOVER

Artificial Gel

Gel nails are for those people who are sensitive to acrylic powder and don’t wish to colour or polish their nails. Gel nails give customers a natural look and give them durability equal to acrylic nails.

DISCOVER

Gel X Extentions

Gel-X extensions have the malleability of a soft gel polish and the length of a press-on or glue-on nail. The extension is made out of gel polish which is applied directly on top of your full natural nail and can be cut and shaped to your preference.
